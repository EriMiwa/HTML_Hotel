Breakfast

Marketa’s Bed and Breakfast believes that breakfast is the most important meal of the day and we just don’t think that a continental breakfast cuts it!
In fact, we start where other places leave off! Mornings at Marketa’s start with home-baked scones or muffins and enough coffee to get you going, and then some! Many of our guests enjoy having this pre-cursor to their gourmet breakfast in the lounge, mingling with other guests, while waiting for their breakfast to be cooked especially for them.

We serve breakfast in the main floor dining room from 8.30AM to 9.30AM. Your gourmet breakfast choice is garnished with fruit and fresh herbs from our garden!
