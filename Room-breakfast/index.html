Breakfast

Marketa’s Bed and Breakfast believes that breakfast is the most important meal of the day and we just don’t think that a continental breakfast cuts it!
In fact, we start where other places leave off! Mornings at Marketa’s start with home-baked scones or muffins and enough coffee to get you going, and then some! Many of our guests enjoy having this pre-cursor to their gourmet breakfast in the lounge, mingling with other guests, while waiting for their breakfast to be cooked especially for them.

We serve breakfast in the main floor dining room from 8.30AM to 9.30AM. Your gourmet breakfast choice is garnished with fruit and fresh herbs from our garden!

Here are some menu favourites.

Eggs Benedict

This standard comes alive with Marketa’s special homemade cheese sauce. Poached eggs over ham on an English crumpet with cheese sauce and garnished with herbs and spices. Fresh fruit on the side.

Eggs Florentine

This simple treat, cooked to perfection. Poached eggs over spinach on an English crumpet with homemade cheese sauce, garnished with herbs and spices. Fresh fruit on the side.

Pancakes

Go on, spoil yourself you deserve it! Pancakes with seasonal fresh fruit, whipped cream and real Canadian Maple syrup.

Montreal Style Bagel

A west-coast favourite’s guaranteed to put a smile on your face! Bagel with cream cheese and smoked salmon, garnished with spices and capers. Fresh fruit on the side.
