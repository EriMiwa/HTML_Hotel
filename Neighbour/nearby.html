Local Dining

Downtown

Il Covo Trattoria

Located in the historic area of downtown and steps from the Inner Harbour, Il Covo Trattoria is a memorable and unique Italian dining experience. Being a Trattoria, we’re the perfect spot for any occasion where you can enjoy casual, rustic Italian cuisine in a warm old world Tuscan ambience. We also offer a beautiful garden patio for outdoor eating in an al fresco Italian style.

106 Superior St., Victoria website

Downtown

Victoria Harbour House Restaurant

Offers international cuisine of the fine quality. Elegant, comfortable it overlooks Victoria Inner Harbour. The service is always excellent. The menu has a wide selection of fine steaks, delicate seafood or rack of lamb roasted to perfection. Just go by and smell it!

607 Oswego St, Victoria website

Steak

The Keg

Serving Victoria for over 25 year right in the heart of downtown, the Keg continues serving the best steak grilled to your exacting taste… and the prime rib slow roasted in famous Keg spice. There is much more on the menu, starting with appetizers, salads, seafood (just imagine steak and lobster). Must not miss the delectable deserts. Enjoy the sunset and the friendly environment. Classic!

500 Fort St. Victoria website

Steak

Nautical Nellies Steak & Seafood House

We are well known for specializing in fresh, delectable seafood specialties and mouth watering premium aged steaks. Nautical Nellies has established itself as a world class steak and seafood house that keeps guests coming back for more.

1001 Wharf St., Victoria website

Italian

Pagliacci's

Extremely popular with the locals, convenient downtown location, 10 min. walk from the Inner Harbour, Pagliacci’s offer a great selection of salads and pasta. Named after your favorite movies and movie stars each meal has a little twist. Half portions are available and even half would fill you up. During the week there is live entertainment. We suggest that you may go early to avoid the line up, they don’t take reservations.

1011 Broad St., Victoria website
